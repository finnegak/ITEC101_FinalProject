    Privacy Concerns in Technology

    With the increasing integration of technology into our daily lives, there's a growing risk of unauthorized access, surveillance, and misuse of sensitive data. Privacy is a fundamental human right and is crucial for individual freedom. The misuse of personal information can lead to identity theft, cyberbullying, discrimination, and other harmful consequences. I feel strongly about privacy because it forms the foundation of freedom and trust. The current impact is evident in numerous data breaches and privacy leaks. In the future, if privacy concerns are not adequately addressed, it could lead to a society where individuals are constantly under surveillance.

    Environmental Impact of Technology

    The environmental impact of technology encompasses the ways in which the production and disposal of technology contribute to the environment. This includes e-waste and energy consumption. This is crucial due to the growing awareness of climate change and the need for sustainable practices. The rapid pace of new technology results in a high turnover of devices. I feel strongly about the environmental impact because the consequences of unchecked technological growth can have long-lasting effects on the planet. Currently, the environmental impact of technology is evident by the carbon footprint due to technology production. In the future, without responsible practices, these issues could worsen, contributing to environmental degradation and climate change.
